# **8.2 High‑Speed Decomposition & Fermentation (24–48 Hours)**

MBT55/HMT238 reduces the traditional composting time (3–6 months) to **24–48 hours**.

### **① Hydrolysis (0–6 hours)**

- Proteins → Amino acids
- Starch → Sugars
- Lipids → Fatty acids
- Cellulose → Glucose

### **② Fermentation & Metabolism (6–18 hours)**

- Organic acid production
- Antimicrobial metabolite generation
- Formation of humic precursors

### **③ Stabilization & Humification (18–24 hours)**

- Formation of humic and fulvic acids
- pH stabilization
- Pathogen elimination (80–100°C fermentation heat)

# **8.3 Upcycling Spent Coffee Grounds (SCG)**

Millions of tons of SCG are produced globally each year.

Traditional problems:

- High moisture → heavy → expensive to transport
- Incineration → CO₂ emissions
- Regulatory barriers to reuse
- Waste costs for cafés and roasters

MBT55/HMT238 converts SCG into **four high‑value resources**.

---

## **① Premium Organic Fertilizer**

MBT55/HMT238 transforms SCG into fertilizer within 24 hours.

### **Nutrient Profile**

- Total nitrogen: 2.5–3.5%
- Available phosphorus: 1.8–2.5%
- Potassium: 1.5–2.0%
- Humic substances: 15–25%
- pH: 6.5–7.0

### **Effects**

- Improved soil fertility
- Increased water retention
- Enhanced microbial diversity
- Higher seedling survival

---

## **② Functional Animal Feed**

SCG contains:

- Polyphenols
- Dietary fiber
- Amino acids
- Trace minerals

MBT55/HMT238 fermentation increases digestibility to **85–92%**.

### **Applications**

- Poultry
- Swine
- Aquaculture (fishmeal alternative)

---

## **③ Biochar**

SCG can be pyrolyzed into biochar.

### **Benefits**

- Long‑term carbon storage (100–500 years)
- Soil amendment
- Microbial carrier for MBT55/HMT238

---

## **④ Water Purification Material**

Humic substances produced by MBT55/HMT238:

- Chelate heavy metals
- Adsorb ammonia and nitrates
- Decompose organic pollutants

### **Applications**

- Aquaculture ponds
- Lakes and rivers
- Agricultural drainage
- Wastewater treatment
