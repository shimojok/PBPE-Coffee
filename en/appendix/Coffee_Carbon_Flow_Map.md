# **1. Overview of Carbon Flows in the Coffee Value Chain**

The coffee life cycle consists of six major stages:

```
1. Cultivation (Farm)
2. Processing (Pulping, Drying)
3. Transport (Farm → Port → Roaster)
4. Roasting (Roaster)
5. Consumption (Cafés & Households)
6. Waste (Spent Coffee Grounds, Pulp, Parchment)
```

PBPE × MBT55/HMT238 adds **carbon value** to every stage.
