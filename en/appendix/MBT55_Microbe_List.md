# **MBT55/HMT238 Microbial Consortium List

— A High‑Diversity, High‑Functionality Ecosystem Engine (120+ Species)**

MBT55/HMT238 is a **high‑diversity microbial consortium** composed of more than 120 species,  
balanced at **55% aerobic : 45% anaerobic**, and designed to function as a  
**multi‑environment, multi‑function ecological engine**.

Its characteristics include:

- **Multi‑functional capabilities** (decomposition, fermentation, pathogen suppression, immune activation)
- **Adaptation across environments** (leaf surface, bark, rhizosphere, gut)
- **24‑hour organic matter decomposition**
- **Humic substance formation (70–85%)**
- **Ecosystem‑level biosecurity (five‑layer model)**

This appendix organizes the major microbial groups that constitute MBT55/HMT238.
